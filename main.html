OSDConf2018
10-11 March

What is it about?


Open Source Developers' Conference is the first of its kind event, solely focused on the Open Source community. We have a lot in store for you whether you're just an amateur developer or a seasoned programmer. Explore and discover new trends in the community, get to know people actively contributing to the community and most importantly go home with more knowledge than you had before. We'll have tech-talks, keynotes from prominent developers, devsprint to discuss and develop new ideas with like-minded people and what not. So, mark your calenders for 10th and 11th of March and don't miss out on this unique event.
